📚 Heat Transfer Curriculum

Interactive Labs & Learning Modules

Welcome to the Heat Transfer Curriculum! Select a lab below to explore fundamental concepts, perform interactive calculations, and test your understanding. Each lab includes theory, calculators, quizzes, and experiments designed for your apparatus.

🔥

Lab 1

Introduction to Heat Transfer

Learn the three fundamental modes of heat transfer: conduction, convection, and radiation. Includes interactive calculators and real-world examples.

⏱️ 45 mins
Topics: Conduction • Convection • Radiation • Formulas
📐

Lab 2

Temperature Gradients

Understand how temperature changes with distance and calculate temperature gradients. Analyze multi-sensor data from your apparatus.

⏱️ 40 mins
Topics: Gradients • Temperature Profiles • Data Analysis
🧱

Lab 3

Thermal Resistance

Explore thermal resistance and R-values. Learn about series and parallel thermal circuits, just like electrical circuits!

⏱️ 50 mins
Topics: R-values • Thermal Circuits • Insulation • Multi-Layer
💨

Lab 4

Forced vs Natural Convection

Compare natural and forced convection heat transfer. Experiment with different fan speeds and observe the effects on cooling rates.

⏱️ 55 mins
Topics: Natural Convection • Forced Convection • h coefficient
📊

Lab 5

Data Analysis & Graphing

Learn professional data analysis techniques: statistics, linear regression, error analysis, and how to write lab reports.

⏱️ 60 mins
Topics: Statistics • Regression • Graphing • Report Writing

💡 Tip: Complete labs in order for the best learning experience!

Each lab includes theory, interactive tools, quizzes, and hands-on experiments.
